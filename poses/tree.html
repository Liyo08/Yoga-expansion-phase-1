Vrikshasana

Tree Pose is a balancing yoga pose that enhances focus, stability, and posture. This pose strengthens the legs,core and spine, while also promoting mental calmness.

How to Do It:

  • Stand tall with feet together, arms by your side.
  • Shift your weight to your left leg and lift your right foot.
  • Place the sole of your right foot on the inner left thigh.
  • Keep your standing leg strong and engage your core.
  • Bring your palms together in a prayer position or raise them overhead.
  • Stay in the pose for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then switch sides.
