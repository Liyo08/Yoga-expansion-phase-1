Adho Mukha Svanasana

it is a rejuvenating yoga posture that strengthens the entire body while improving flexibility. It helps stretch the spine, hamstrings, and calves, while also strengthening the arms and shoulders.

How to Do It:

  • Get on your hands & knees with wrists under shoulders & knees under hips.
  • Exhale & raise your hips toward the ceiling, straightening your legs.
  • Spread your fingers wide and press your palms firmly into the mat.
  • Ensure a straight back, with your head between your arms, looking towards your feet.
  • Stay in the pose for 30 sec to 1 min, then release by lowering your knees into Childs Pose.
