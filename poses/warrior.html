Virabhadrasana

Description: Warrior Pose is a powerful standing yoga pose that builds strength, endurance, and stability. It strengthens the legs, arms, and core, improves balance, and enhances focus.

How to Do It:

  • Stand tall with feet together and arms at your sides.
  • Take a big step back with your right foot, keeping your left foot forward.
  • Lower your hips so your left knee forms a 90-degree angle over your ankle.
  • Straighten your right leg and press your back heel into the floor.
  • Stretch both arms overhead, keeping shoulders relaxed.
  • Maintain for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then switch sides.
